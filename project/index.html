Our Roles at Wine Binding

At Wine Binding Company, each member plays a vital role in bringing our vision to life:

  • Founder & Visionary: Guides the company mission and values.
  • Wine Curator: Selects and maintains our finest wine collection.
  • Customer Care Team: Ensures every guest feels welcome and valued.
  • Community Liaison: Connects our work to Epworth heritage and culture.

Our Collection

At Wine Binding Company, our collection is more than bottles — it's a story of heritage, craftsmanship, and passion. Each wine is carefully selected to reflect the richness of our culture and the artistry of winemaking.
